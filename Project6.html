Project 6 – Southern Ocean Winds and Overturning

What controls the Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation (AMOC)?
  • Read Toggweiler & Samuels 1995
  • Re-revaluate their claims (ie AMOC ~ SO winds) with a model that uses isopycnal diffusion (Veros) instead of horizontal diffusion
    • Hint: doubling taux south of 12S is done by vs.surface_taux[:, vs.yt < -12] *= 2
  • Is the SO dependence of the AMOC stronger or weaker in Veros?
